Chicken Marsala

Description

This recipe for chicken marsala is absolutely delicious. The sauce made of marsala wine and heavy cream is the perfect flavor to pair with the chicken breasts and bella mushrooms. You can put it all on mashed potatoes or buttered noodles for the perfect meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 3 tbls all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbls olive oil
  • 3 tbls butter
  • 8 oz sliced bella mushrooms
  • 3 tbls finely chopped shallots
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2/3 cup chicken broth
  • 2/3 cup Marsala wine
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tsp fresh thyme

Steps

  1. Heat the oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place the chicken in the pan and cook until golden and barely cooked through.
  2. Remove the chicken from the pan and melt the remaining tablespoon of butter in the pan. Add the mushrooms and cook until they begin to brown.
  3. Add the shallots, garlic, and salt and cook for one more minute. Add the broth, Marsala, heavy cream, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Scrape any brown bits from the pan to incorporate in the sauce. Bring the liquid to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium and gently boil, uncovered, until the sauce is slightly thickened and darker in color.
  5. Add the chicken back to the pan. Reduce the heat to low and simmer until the chicken is warmed through.
  6. Serve!
