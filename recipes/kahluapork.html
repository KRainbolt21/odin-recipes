Kahlua Pork

Description

This pork has really good flavor and is super easy to make! You can simply put it in a slow cooker in the morning with the other ingredients and it will be waiting for you at the end of the day, all ready to eat.

Ingredients

  • 6-8 pounds pork shoulder
  • 1 tbls liquid smoke
  • 2-3 tsp red Hawaiian salt

Steps

  1. Rinse and pat the pork dry with a paper towel and place in the slow cooker.
  2. Pierce the pork all over with a fork, pour the liquid smoke over the top, and sprinkle with the Hawaiian salt.
  3. Place the lid of the slow cooker on and let cook for 8-12 hours on low.
  4. Remove 2 cups of liquid and set aside. Shred the pork with forks and then add some of the liquid back in to keep the pork from drying out.
